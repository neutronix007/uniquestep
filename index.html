Unique Step UK

Unique Step UK Ltd is a care company that specialises in providing personalised domiciliary care services to individuals with a wide range of disabilities. We are committed to providing compassionate homecare services across Thurrock and neighbouring areas. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing high-quality care and companionship to those in need. We offer a range of services, including Domiciliary and companionship care, Supported Living and live-in care, tailored to meet individual needs.

We are also committed to fostering opportunities, inclusion, and growth, aiming to enhance the lives of adults with physical disabilities, mental health conditions and additional needs.

Unique Step UK Ltd is proud to be approved by the Care Quality Commission, ensuring the highest standards of care. Contact us today to discuss how we can assist you.
