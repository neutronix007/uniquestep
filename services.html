Homecare/Domiciliary Services

Our home care service is tailored to meet the needs of service users who live at home but need care and support to maintain quality of life. This service applies to both young people and adults.

LIVE-IN CARE

Unique Step UK offers a 24/7 live-in care service for clients in Thurrock, Essex and Surrounding areas, staffed by experienced, highly trained carers who assist with daily life tasks such as personal care and housekeeping.

Respite Services

At Unique Step UK, we understand the importance of taking a break. Our respite services provide temporary relief for families and caregivers, ensuring that your loved ones receive the highest quality care in a safe and supportive environment. Whether it’s for a few hours, a day, or an extended period, our dedicated team is here to offer personalised care tailored to individual needs. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that your loved ones are in good hands, allowing you to recharge and take care of yourself.

Supported Living

Our supported Living service provides support for service users in their own home but require the support of staff to live independently and maintain quality of life. This service applies to young people and adults.

Some of the supports provided include the followings:

  • Support with accessing the community
  • Supporting with attending appointments
  • Medication support and management
  • Food preparation and assistance with meals with a focus on healthy eating

Some of the supports provided include the followings:

  • Personal Care
  • ADHD
  • Challenging Behaviours
  • Mental Health Conditions

Some of the service users in this category that we support have the following needs:

ADHD

Challenging Behaviours

Dementia

Nursing Care

Palliative Care

Substenance Misuse

Mental Health Issues
